The Best Movies According to Shev

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Fences

This movie beautifully and sadly puts in context how our environment shapes our reality, regardless of how noble our parents'intentions are. It is an eye opener of sorts that deeply calls for introspection and self-work.

2. The Pursuit of Happyness

This movie looks at the American Dream from a minority perspective and gives insight into the costs one has to pay and the losts one has to bear in the pursuit of one's dreams. It's filled with uplifting nuggets that inspires the viewer to be resilient in the pursuit of their dreams and eventually they too will get their own little slice of "happyness".

3. Men of Honor

This movie is a rousing service drama, a true story about honor, courage, determination, and fighting injustice. All in honor of serving your country and fellow man.
